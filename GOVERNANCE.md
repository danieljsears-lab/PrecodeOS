## Governance Model

Precode OS uses a benevolent founder-maintainer model.

Dan Sears / Recode sets project direction, accepts or rejects contributions, resolves roadmap disputes, and protects Precode OS's purpose: helping non-technical solo builders use AI coding agents with guided workflow, scope control, evidence, and human approval.

Maintainer roadmap direction is recorded in `maintainer/PRECODE-ROADMAP.md`; official authority remains Dan Sears / Recode.

## Contribution Authority

Contributors may propose issues, documentation, code, tests, examples, workflows, or protocols. Contribution does not create governance rights, trademark rights, roadmap authority, maintainer status, or authority to represent official Precode OS.

Maintainer review is required before any contribution changes:

- governance, contribution, trademark, license, or provenance policy
- public positioning or official project identity
- active memory rules
- core workflow semantics
- generated-output demotion
- beginner-facing safety language

## Forks And Succession

Forks are allowed under Apache-2.0. Forks and derivatives must follow `TRADEMARK.md` when naming or presenting themselves.

Official Precode OS direction and brand remain governed by Dan Sears / Recode unless maintainer authority is explicitly delegated in writing.
